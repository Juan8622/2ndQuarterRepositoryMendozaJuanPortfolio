CARD GUIDE

Card types Examples Description
Win conditions This is a win condition, these cards target buildings, and they are mainly used to do damage to towers.
Aerial Cards Aerial cards, these are cards that can't be hit by ground troops(except for ranged troops like witch, wizard, or musk), which makes it crucial in a deck to add defense against strong cards..
Champion Cards Champion cards, only one is allowed per deck and they have a special ability, they are cards that are unlocked later on in the clash royale arenas, and they are also extremely usefull and usually result in positive elixir trades.
Cycle Cards Cycle cards, these cards are low elixir cards that help you cycle through a deck faster.
Range Cards Range cards, these are cards that specialize in ranged damage/damage from afar.
Splash Damage Cards Splash damage cards, cards that do a lot of aoe and counter swarm cards like skeleton army.
Swarms Swarms are units that do a lot of damage, but usually have low health, making them vulnerable to spells or area of effect damage.
Spells Spells, these cards are used for mainly splash damage, or for giving buffs, or for giving other special effects.

Meta Cards for Each category

Win Conditions:

Royal Hogs

Royal Hogs is a really good one, since it is very good against single defense cards like Musk and Inferno Tower, and it is also a great win condition if paired with a spell.

Hog Rider

Hog Rider is a really good win condition. It is fast, does a ton of damage, cheap elixir, can only be countered by swarm cards, but if it is paired with a spell, it is basically a free tower down.

Balloon

Balloon is a great win condition for dealing damage, and can only be countered by buildings and aerial defense cards, and almost ALWAYS results in positive elixir trades if played correctly.

Swarms:

Skeleton Army

Skeleton Army is a really good swarm card, since it does a ton of damage, but can be countered with spells and aoe cards. The new evolution version of this card makes it meta.

Goblin Gang

Goblin Gang is a good swarm card, with high damage, has some health, and is sort of cheap. It is good for defense, and for support on offense.

Minion Horde

Really good aerial swarm card, great for defense, but can be countered by spells.

Champions:

Golden Knight

Insanely good ability, great damage. Probably one of the best champions to ever exist in this game.

Mighty Miner

Mighty Miner, basically miner but insanelly buffed with great defensive ability. It is great for baiting out defensive troops and countering on the other side. If you use this right you could probably get half a tower down.

Boss Bandit

My personal favorite champion. This champion has a really good dash ability, great for damage on the tower, or on high hp troops, great overall champion.

Cycle Cards:

Ice Spirit

Ice Spirit is my personal cycle card. Great defense, good for support, good aoe and freeze ability.

Skeletons

ELITE defense. Great for kiting decks, great for chip damage on troops, and maybe even towers. The evo makes this card a guaranteed positive elixir trade card.

Fire Spirit

Great for aoe damage, support, and cycling, not too much to say about it though.

Range Cards:

Musketeer

AWESOME card. INSANE DEFENSE, this card is almost used in EVERY meta deck. Great damage, good range, good health, and paired with a nice win condition, you could possibly tower down.

Wizard

INSANELY good splash damage, great for tower damage too. Great for defense and offense support. Highly counterable with musk, or any non-swarm high health card.

Dart Goblin

Insanely great damage output, but loggable. Has really low health, but if placed correctly it can carry a defense and offense.

Spells:

Fireball

Great AOE, lowish elixir, can wipeout high health troops. Great for supporting tanky win conditions.

Arrows

Awesome damage, low elixir, great for obliterating swarms. Easy positive elixir trades.

Log

The GOAT spell. Low elixir cost, insane value. Does a ton of damage for swarms, insanely good for support and defense.

Aerial Cards:

Baby Dragon

The evo makes it op support. But in base its splash and indiv damage is great for defense. It can be used as a support card for offense, and it can also solo defenses.

Minions

Almost in every meta deck, great for defense, but has really low health and can be countered by spells.

Inferno Dragon

Can be countered by swarm cards or skeletons or any card that has low health and is high in number. But INSANELY great for dealing with tanky attackers, and when locked on to a tower, you can possibly get a tower down.

Splash Damage Cards:

Valkyrie

Awesome card, really good for dealing with swarms, the evo makes it really good too. Great for defense and support.

Executioner

Evo makes it crazy. Good offense, decent defense. Deals with swarm cards like skarmy really well.

Dark Prince

GREAT ATTACK, deals with swarms like crazy, and is cheap elixir. Can do crazy good tower damage, or could be a good support if you are running something like a PEKKA deck.
